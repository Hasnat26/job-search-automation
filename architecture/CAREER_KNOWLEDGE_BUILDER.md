# Career Knowledge Builder Architecture

## Status

Draft v0.1

---

## Purpose

The Career Knowledge Builder is the core intelligence engine of the Career Operating System (Career OS).

Its responsibility is to transform multiple career documents into a single trusted Master Career Knowledge Base.

Unlike a traditional Master CV, the system stores atomic Career Facts, preserves provenance, resolves conflicts, and constructs canonical career objects that become the single source of truth for all downstream modules.
